STEP B10 Identity Verification — Company Management Verify
Each member of company management must complete identity verification. Automated — no calls required. Powered by Signicat.
📧
Send KYC invite emails
For each management person (except the applicant), an email is sent with a secure KYC link. The applicant completes verification inline via the "Start" button. Resend is available for any person.
🪪
Each person completes:
Document photo (passport, national ID, or driving licence) + liveness check selfie. Automated review, usually seconds.
The "Continue without verifying" button is available — applicant can proceed and let others complete verification asynchronously.
